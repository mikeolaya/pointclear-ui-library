Color Palette

PointClear has three primary brand colors—midnight blue, clear blue and cerise red— which make up roughly 80 percent of its visual identity. They have been carefully chosen to represent PointClear’s key characteristics: knowledge, healthcare focus and passion for innovation.

While blues convey stability and trust, the unique tones in combination with cerise red creates a fresh and energetic visual display. The cerise red adds a daring confidence that makes the brand memorable.

Please make sure all print files are set to CMYK or Spot Colors. The RGB and Web colors are reserved for online use only.

Note that colors vary when printed on various paper stocks. Color variation is also unavoidable on different computer monitors.

We recommend always requesting a print proof to ensure colors are consistent.
