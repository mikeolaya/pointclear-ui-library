Logo Introduction

Upon first glance the logo mark may look like a complex shape, but upon further study the meaning is clear. The descender of the “p” completes the simple 3D box. This box frame represents the foundation of PointClear’s soft ware development.

The mark has a technological feel, while the lowercase customized font in the logo is friendly and approachable. This balance is refl ective of PointClear’s team and culture.

Primary Logo

The clear space is equal to the height of the “p” from the top of the ascender to the bottom of the descender.

The minimum recommended size of the logo is larger when displayed with the tagline than without. When a tagline is used, it is recommended that the logo go no smaller than 1.75” wide.

Without the tagline the logo can go as small as 1” wide.

If necessary, the PointClear logo can be reversed out of any of its primary brand colors. It can also be reversed out of black, if necessary.

Secondary Logo

The PointClear logo without the mark is a secondary option. The primary logo is preferred, but there may be rare instances when the name works best alone.

The minimum recommended size of the logo is larger when displayed with the tagline than without. When a tagline is used, it is recommended that the logo be no smaller than 1.35” wide.

And when it is used without the tagline it can goes as small as .77” wide.

The logo can be reversed out of any of its primary brand colors. It can also be reversed out of black, if necessary.

Logo Mark

The logo mark can be carefully used in isolation. For example: the mark could be used as a social media avatar where the name of the company sits adjacent to the mark.

The mark might also be used as a secondary icon on the back of a data sheet, white paper, pocket folder or some other collateral where the primary logo has already been positioned prominently on the front. Use creative license, but be careful not to assume the audience will recognize PointClear with the mark alone.

The logo mark can be displayed (positive or reverse) in any tint of any brand color.

Logo Variations

All versions of the PointClear logo, including the logo mark in isolation, can be used in black and white, if necessary. The mark can also be reversed out of black and used as grey tone watermarks.

Logo's Don't

Do not stretch the logo in any way. It should always be scaled proportionately.

Never use the logo in any other color besides PointClear brand colors.

Do not position the logo over a busy photograph. As seen on previous pages, there should always be plenty of clear space around the logo.

Never edit the type treatment of the logo or tagline. The typography of the PointClear logo has been customized. The artwork will be supplied as outlined vector art, therefore the type of the company name and/or tagline should never be edited.

While the logo mark may be used on its own, it should always be in the exact same position—to the left of the company name—when used together.
