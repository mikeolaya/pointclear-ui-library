Our Design Principles

Welcoming. Approachable. Confident. Meaningful

PointClear’s design principles are the fundamental attributes we seek to achieve in all elements of the User Experience. These principles drive decisions for the design, technology and business decisions we make on behalf of our clients.

Welcoming

The experiences we craft for clients are pleasing and inviting. We keep our experiences simple and straightforward, so that users can easily get started and spend their time doing rather than learning.

Guiding Lights

  • Simple
  • Intuitive
  • Easy-to-use
  • Guided

Approachable

The experiences we craft for clients are friendly and attainable. Our experiences keep our users engaged and delighted, so that they feel comfortable coming back for more.

Guiding Lights

  • Clarify Complexity

Confident

Our experiences help guide and direct users through information to complete their goals. We create intuitive designs that establish clear metaphors and directions for user action.

Guiding Lights

  • Defer to Content

Meaningful

Our experiences speak to people’s needs and generate value for both our clients and users. We empower and delight users through our solutions, helping them become more efficient, intelligent and fun.

Guiding Lights

  • Make the Useful Beautiful
