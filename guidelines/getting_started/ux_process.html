Our UX Process

Discover. Design. Create. Evaluate. Repeat as necessary.

Discover

Explore who people really are and how they think, feel and move through life. This is how we discover the right solution:

DIRECTIONS:

  • Talk to people.
  • Identify & describe whom you want to help.
  • Research. Study. Think. Begin to empathize.
  • Identify & describe how you will help her or him.
  • Hypothesize.
  • Lastly, tell us the story.

Design

Transform what you have learned about people into product concepts and share those stories with people. This is how we design the right features:

DIRECTIONS:

  • Sketch wildly and playfully.
  • Create experiences.
  • Play with colors.
  • Wireframe prototypes together.
  • Hone your ideas.
  • Last, but not Sell your ideas - I mean really, pitch them…

Create

Collaborate to refine the details and bring your creation to life! This is how we create the right plan forward.

DIRECTIONS:

  • Discuss the Vision.
  • Prioritize ruthlessly.
  • Know that the devil is in the details, and that there are lots of them.
  • Collaborate and negotiate relentlessly.
  • Be helpful and adaptable.
  • Lastly, play… Play with your solution throughout.

Evaluate

Understand how your creation will impact the world by seeing how people will actually experience it. This is how we evaluate the right product.

DIRECTIONS:

  • Leave your ego at the door.
  • Don’t talk. Listen and observe fastidiously.
  • Write down everything you see, feel, hear, smell, taste and/or divine.
  • Grade yourself.
  • Present and discuss your observations.
  • Lastly, decide if you should walk away…
